More detailed information and probabilities

  • Common Carp - Found Almost Everywhere
  • Northern Pike - Found in Meteor Lake
  • Yellow Perch - Found in Meteor Lake
  • Rainbow Trout - Found in the Ocean and Meteor Lake
  • Brook Trout - Found in the summit pond and in the Ocean
  • White Bass - Found in cold ponds
  • Salmon - Found at the foot of waterfalls
  • Catfish - Found in all rivers
  • Spotted Brook Trout - Found in Oceans or in the bay near beachstickball
  • Burbot - Found in fast rivers
  • Crayfish - Found in tiny ponds
  • Pumpkinseed - Found in ponds with lillies
  • Bluegill - Found in the bay near the sandcastle frog
  • White Perch - Found in calm rivers
